When someone becomes a celebrity, they usually have one of two choices. They can stay humble and remember where they came from... or they can become a massive star who doesn't ever look back. While Taylor Russell appears to be dating Harry Styles, she does seem to be in the first category. The actress was also linked to Timothee Chalamet after the two starred in the horror movie Bones And All.

Taylor Russell has been on an intense ride since fans watched Bones And All and realized what an amazing talent she is. But has the fame gone to her head? Does she still remember and talk about her childhood in Vancouver? And has she stayed in touch with the friends she has made throughout her life?

Has Taylor Russell Stayed Close With Her Friends From Vancouver?

After co-starring in Bones In All with Timothee Chalamet, Taylor Russell has proven that she is a perfect fit for horror movies. Besides the film about two cannibals, Russell played Zoey Davis in the creepy 2019 movie Escape Room and the sequel, Escape Room: Tournament of Champions, which was released in 2021.

Before she became famous, Taylor Russell grew up in Vancouver, British Columbia. In an interview with Harper's Bazaar, she explained that she was born in Deep Cove, an area located in North Vancouver. She and her parents began living in Toronto when she was six years old. She told the reporter that she doesn't visit Deep Cove. It's too hard because it has been altered a lot because of how gentrified it's become.

Taylor Russell was once good friends with Emilija Baranac, a 28-year-old actress who is also from Vancouver. While she played Heidi in three episodes of the Charmed reboot, she is famous for playing Genevieve in the To All The Boys I've Loved Before Netflix movies. Baranac is also known for playing Midge in Riverdale and her role on Fakes.

While neither actress has said that they are still friends, they follow one another on social media, which suggests that they keep in touch in some way, shape or form.

Back in 2018, Baranac and Russell did a photo shoot with Reema Ismail, a photographer living in Vancouver. They looked like they had a lot of fun. It's possible that they are still friends, and Russell just doesn't talk about it.

Taylor Russell also starred in the TV series Lost In Space, which began streaming on Netflix in 2018 and ended in 2021. Based on the show from 1965, Lost In Space is about the Robinsons who travel through space. Russell played the oldest sibling Judy Robinson, who is one of the smartest characters on the show.

Lost In Space was filmed in Vancouver, and also starred Mina Sundwall, Paker Posey, Maxwell Jenkins, Toby Stephens, and Molly Parker. Jenkins was really young at the time that the show filmed, and he's only 18 years now.

It seems that Russell has kept in touch with her co-stars from Lost In Space, which suggests that she does like remembering her life before she became a huge celebrity.

Earlier in 2023, Redditor @Butterscotchtape_ posted some adorable photos of Russell and Jenkins having fun together in the SubReddit called "Taylor Russell Updates." The fan wrote, "Taylor Russell with her Lost In Space costar Maxwell Jenkins." It's unclear when this photo was taken, but the actors looked excited to be together, which suggests that they bonded.
