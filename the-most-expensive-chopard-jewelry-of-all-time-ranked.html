Chopard is a Swiss luxury watch and jewelry manufacturer synonymous with elegance, sophistication, and opulence since its inception in 1860. Founded by Louis-Ulysse Chopard, the brand has consistently pushed the boundaries of design and craftsmanship, creating some of the most exquisite and expensive pieces of jewelry and timepieces the world has ever seen. With a wide range of collections that cater to different tastes and styles, Chopard has become a favorite among discerning customers who appreciate the brand's blend of tradition and innovation.

From dazzling watches to elegant jewelry, Chopard is the maker of some of the most expensive accessories in the world, some costing millions of dollars.

Chopard Imperiale Automatic 36 mm White Gold Watch - $65,740

The Chopard Imperiale Automatic 36 mm White Gold Watch is a stunning example of the brand's expertise in watchmaking. This timepiece features an 18-carat white gold case adorned with multiple diamonds on the bezel and lugs. Diamond-set hour markers embellish the mother-of-pearl dial, while the white gold bracelet adds a touch of sophistication. With its automatic movement and water resistance of up to 50 meters, as per Swiss Luxury, this watch is a luxurious accessory and a reliable timekeeping instrument.

Chopard Imperiale Automatic 40 mm White Gold Watch - $90,600

Taking the elegance of the Imperiale collection a step further, the Chopard Imperiale Automatic 40 mm White Gold Watch boasts a larger case size and an even more lavish design. Several diamonds encrust the 18-carat white gold case, while the dial features an intricate guilloché pattern and diamond-set hour markers. The white gold bracelet, adorned with exquisite diamonds, completes the opulent look of this timepiece, taking its price up to a whopping $90,600.

Chopard Xtravaganza XL Rose Gold Watch - $ 92,740

The Chopard Xtravaganza XL Rose Gold Watch is a testament to the brand's creativity and innovation. This exquisite timepiece features a unique design, with an oversized 18-carat rose gold case and a diamond-studded bezel set. The dial is made of white mother-of-pearl, and diamonds adorn the rose gold bracelet, creating a harmonious blend of materials and colors. This watch perfectly exemplifies Chopard's ability to combine unconventional design with exceptional craftsmanship.

Chopard World’s Most Expensive Sunglasses - $ 400,000

Chopard's foray into luxury eyewear resulted in the creation of the world's most expensive sunglasses, priced at a staggering $400,000. These exclusive shades feature a 24-carat gold frame, with the Chopard logo embellished with 51 full-cut River diamonds, as mentioned by Luxuo. The arms of the sunglasses consist of 60 grams of gold, making these sunglasses not only a fashion statement but also a symbol of luxury and extravagance.

Chopard Secret Watch - $508,000

The Chopard Secret Watch is a magnificent piece of jewelry that cleverly conceals a timepiece within its design. Crafted from 18-carat white gold, this exquisite bracelet features a stunning array of diamonds and sapphires totaling over 163 carats. The hidden watch is revealed by sliding the central sapphire, showcasing Chopard's unparalleled mastery of manufacturing intricate timepieces. This watch is priced at over half a million dollars, with only two units ever made.

Chopard Super Ice Cube Watch - $1.1 million

The Chopard Super Ice Cube Watch is a breathtaking timepiece that takes luxury to a whole new level. This extraordinary watch features a case and bracelet made entirely of 18-carat white gold, set with numerous small diamonds totaling almost 66 carats. Several diamonds decorate the dial, making this watch a true work of art while increasing its price to over a million dollars.

Chopard Diamond and Emerald Necklace - $3 million

This magnificent Chopard Diamond and Emerald Necklace represents the brand's legacy of creating exquisite jewelry. The necklace features a stunning combination of 191 carats of pear-shaped Colombian emeralds and 16 carats of brilliant-cut diamonds, all set in 18-karat white gold, as reported by Success Story. The intricate design and use of precious gemstones make this necklace a true showstopper.

Chopard Blue Diamond Ring - $16.26 million

The Chopard Blue Diamond Ring is an extraordinary piece of jewelry that showcases the brand's commitment to using only the finest materials while also being one of the world’s most expensive Jewelries. This ring features a breathtaking 9-carat, oval-shaped blue diamond surrounded by a halo of white diamonds as per Coloured Stones. The 18-karat white gold setting adds to the ring's elegance, making it an exceptional piece with a humongous price tag of over $16 Million.

Chopard 201-Carat Watch - $25 million

The Chopard 201-Carat Watch is the epitome of luxury and extravagance. This one-of-a-kind timepiece features a stunning array of 874 diamonds, totaling an incredible 201 carats. Given the watch's unique design, the diamonds are showcased in a mesmerizing, flower-like arrangement. The quartz movement ensures accuracy, while the unparalleled use of precious gemstones represents the peak of craftsmanship.

Chopard's dedication to craftsmanship, innovation, and the use of precious materials has solidified its reputation as a leading luxury brand. From diamond-encrusted watches to the world's most expensive sunglasses, Chopard continues to push the boundaries of luxury and elegance.
