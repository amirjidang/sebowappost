Are Uche And Clinton Of 'Are You The One' Still Together? Here's What Their Relationship Looks Like

August 2024 · 5 minute read

For eight seasons, the reality TV show, Are You The One?, has paired two different cast members as a perfect match based on answers they give in a predetermined interview. This often leads to people falling in love and starting a life together as a couple. For example, Amber Lee and Ethan Diamond met and fell in love on Are You The One?, and they are currently married.

Uche Nwosu and Clinton Moxam are among those who can certify that finding love on a reality show is actually possible. The two met during season 6 of Are You The One?, fell in love and formed a powerful connection. They proved they were a perfect couple when they officially tied the knot in September 2021. Despite the discouraging news that the computer algorithm had not paired Uche and Clinton as a "prefect match" for each other on the show, the two were able to form one of the strongest connections ever in the show.

8 Uche Nwosu And Clinton Moxam Moved In Together After The Series

While still shooting the sixth season, Uche and Clinton resided in separate locations, Michigan and Florida, respectively. After the shoot, Uche moved to Florida to stay with her new boyfriend.

Later in November 2017, during the Are You The One? reunion episode, the two confirmed that they had moved in together. The couple revealed to their fans that, through cohabitation, they were able to open up about each other's personal life and hence build up trust and solidify their relationship.

7 Uche Nwosu And Clinton Moxam's Engagement

Salutes to Clinton Moxam for letting Uche and the whole world know that she is the one. According to Moxam, it took them three years to get engaged. On an Instagram post, Clinton Moxam shared his beautiful proposal to the bride-to-be on a beach during a vacation. Uche, on her Instagram, posted and quoted, "Still speechless. I honestly didn’t think love like this existed. My lover. My fiancé, I can’t wait to be your wife."

6 Uche Nwosu And Clinton Moxam's Current Status

After dating for more than three years, Clinton Moxam and Uche Nwosu were ready to commit as husband and wife. Fifteen months after they confirmed their engagement, the couple was united in wedlock, a stunning event at the heart of Chicago, at Palmer House, Hilton Hotel.

Also, the newly-weds honored their parents of Nigerian and Jamaican origins with special ceremonies dedicated to them before the official ceremony. With a breathtaking fashion and location, Clinton and Uche finally had the wedding of their dreams.

5 Uche Nwosu and Clinton Moxam Host A YouTube Channel Together

Uche Nwosu and Clinton Moxam opened a YouTube channel named "Uche & Clinton" over three years ago. They dedicated it to their fans as a platform for sharing different aspects of their lives through vlogs.

With over 40,000 subscribers on board, the two can now engage their fans. On the channel, they also answer questions from their fans about their personal life, such as daily routines.

4 Clinton Moxam And Uche Nwosu Now Live In Los Angeles

According to Over The Moon, Clinton Moxam And Uche Nwosu lived in Florida when they decided to move in together. A few months later, they agreed to move to L.A. where they have stayed ever since.

In an interview published on Over The Moon, Clinton explained how the COVID-19 situation in Los Angeles affected his proposal plans. “Originally, I wanted to propose to Uche with family members around in New Orleans, where we first met, but we were stuck in L.A. and none of our family would be able to attend,” Maxom said.

3 Uche Nwosu Loves Vacations

Many fans who follow Uche Nwosu on her social media platforms would probably agree that she enjoys the outdoor life. The numerous photos and videos of hotels and beaches she posts on her Instagram say it all about her.

Occasionally, taking vacations together has helped the connection between Uche and Clinton grow stronger. It has glued them together for four years, contrary to the norm, as many reality TV couples break up.

2 Clinton Moxam And Uche Nwosu's Dog

A dog became the first addition to the lives of Clinton Moxam And Uche Nwosu. In a video on their YouTube channel, Uche explained that she always wanted to have a dog but as long as she was living with her mother, that was never going to happen.

She took it to her Instagram page to show how much she loved her pet and even captioned that she is a great dog mom.

1 Clinton Moxam Is A Physical Trainer

Clinton Moxam loves working out. He uses all his social media platform to share tips on how to train effectively. He is also a model, and in modeling, the body physique is a significant determinant of one's performance on stage.

On his website, The Very Day Sweat, Clinton has uploaded plenty of workout videos. He trains people on how different workouts target different body muscles such as chest, legs, back, among others. Also, on several occasions, Clinton hits the gym with his wife, Uche.
