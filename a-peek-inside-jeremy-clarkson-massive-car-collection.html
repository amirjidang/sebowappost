A Peek Inside Jeremy Clarkson Massive Car Collection

Jeremy Clarkson is a car enthusiast, televisi personality, and journalist who has amassed an impressive collection of cars over the years. As one of the hosts of the popular BBC television show Top Gear, Clarkson has been able to test and review some of the world's most exotic and expensive cars while garnering a massive net worth of $70 Million. Over the years, Clarkson has collected a range of cars, including vintage cars and modern state-of-the-art supercars.

Jeremy Clarkson’s drool-worthy car collection includes some of the biggest luxury car brands in the world, including Ferrari, BMW, Mercedes, Lamborghini, McLaren, and so on.

Alfa Romeo Alfetta GTV6 - $30,000

While not the most expensive car in his collection, the Alfa Romeo Alfetta GTV6 is a special car for Jeremy Clarkson. The Alfa Romeo has been among Clarkson’s favorites ever since he got his first car in the form of a 1969 Ford Cortina, as reported by GT. This car features a 2.5-liter V6 engine that produces 158 horsepower and 152 lb-ft of torque.

Ferrari F355 - $95,000

Jeremy Clarkson first test-drove a Ferrari F355 in an episode of Top Gear, and he liked the car so much that he got one for himself, as per Hotcars. The Ferrari F355 is a classic car that was produced in the mid-1990s. It features a 3.5-liter V8 engine that produces 375 horsepower. It can accelerate from 0 to 60 MPH in just 4.6 seconds and has a top speed of 183 MPH. The car is currently priced at $95,000.

Mercedes-Benz CLK63 AMG Black Series - $135,000

Jeremy Clarkson gifted himself a Mercedes-Benz CLK63 AMG Black Series in 2007, as per CARHP. The CLK63 AMG is a high-performance version of the popular CLK-Class luxury coupe. It features a hand-built 6.2-liter V8 engine that produces a whopping 500 horsepower and 465 lb-ft of torque. The engine is mated to a seven-speed automatic transmission that can shift in just 100 milliseconds, providing lightning-fast acceleration. The CLK63 AMG Black Series can accelerate from 0 to 60 MPH in a meager 4.1 seconds and has a top speed of 186 MPH.

Bentley Continental GT - $202,500

This particular car in Jeremy Clarkson’s collection has quite the backstory. The Bentley Continental GT was initially purchased by The Grand Tour team for use in an episode shot in Madagascar. In preparation for the shoot, the team went all out and heavily modified the grand tourer with lift kits, a roll cage, snorkels, off-road tires, and other accessories, transforming it into an absolute beast. After the shoot, Jeremy Clarkson was so impressed by the Bentley that he decided to keep it for himself and was spotted driving it around his farm. Under the hood of Jeremy Clarkson’s modified Continental GT is a twin-turbocharged, 4.0-liter V8 engine that delivers an impressive 542 horsepower.

Mercedes-Benz SLS AMG Roadster - $213,000

The Mercedes-Benz SLS AMG Roadster is an exquisite high-performance convertible sports car bought by Jeremy Clarkson in 2011. The roadster’s hand-built 6.2-liter V8 engine is a masterpiece of engineering, producing a staggering 591 horsepower and 479 lb-ft of torque per AutoEvolution. Mated to a seven-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission, the SLS AMG Roadster can accelerate from 0 to 60 MPH in just 3.7 seconds, making it one of the fastest cars on the road.

Lamborghini Gallardo Spyder - $225,400

The Lamborghini Gallardo Spyder is another supercar in Clarkson's collection, which he bought after test-driving it in Top Gear. This convertible supercar is powered by a 5.2-liter V10 engine that produces a staggering 562 horsepower and an enormous 398 lb-ft of torque. It can accelerate from 0 to 60 MPH in just 3.2 seconds and can zoom to a top speed of 201 MPH. Clarkson reportedly parted ways with the Spyder in 2017.

McLaren 675LT - $349,500

Jeremy Clarkson owns an exquisite bright-orange McLaren 675LT. The 675LT is a high-performance version of the already impressive 650S. This car is equipped with a seven-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission, providing lightning-fast gear changes powered by a 3.8-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine that produces a massive 666 horsepower and 516 lb-ft of torque. It can accelerate from 0 to 60 MPH in just 2.8 seconds and has a top speed of 205 MPH.

Ford GT - $366,947

The most expensive car on this list, the Ford GT, is a supercar built to compete with Ferrari and Lamborghini. This car is powered by a 5.4-liter supercharged V8 engine that produces 550 horsepower and can easily zoom from 0 to 60 MPH in just 3.3 seconds, reaching a top speed of 205 MPH. Jeremy Clarkson went a bit off-track and got himself a 2005 Ford GT but had quite the hustle adjusting to it. The car's build did not suit Clarkson’s stature, and there were anomalies in the electronics, too, which made him return the car to Ford. But Clarkson being Clarkson, bought back the car yet again, eventually selling it off after a few years.

Jeremy Clarkson's massive car collection is a testament to his love of cars and appreciation for engineering and design. These eight cars are some of the most expensive and impressive cars in his collection, with the Ford GT standing out as the most expensive, with a massive price tag of $366,947. Clarkson's collection is a treasure trove for car enthusiasts, and it will continue to inspire and awe for years to come.
