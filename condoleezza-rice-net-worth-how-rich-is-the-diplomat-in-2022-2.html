Condoleezza Rice Net Worth: How Rich Is the Diplomat in 2022?

August 2024 · 3 minute read

Rice is an American diplomat and political scientist best known for serving as the U.S. secretary of state from 2005-2009. The Condoleezza Rice net worth figure is $10 million.

Early Life and Bio

Condoleezza Rice was born in November of 1954 in Birmingham, Alabama to parents John and Angelena (née Ray). Her father was a high school guidance counselor and dean of students at Stillman College, while his mother was a high school science, music, and oratory teacher.

Meanwhile, Rice began to learn French, music, figure skating, and ballet at the age of three.

As far as education goes, Condoleezza attended an all-girls Catholic high school named St. Mary’s Academy in Denver, Colorado. Then, she enrolled at the University of Denver.

There, Rice earned a B.A., cum laude, in political science in 1974. She later attained a master’s degree in political science from the University of Notre Dame.

Condoleezza Rice Net Worth and Career

Rice officially got into politics in 1986, serving as special assistant to the director of the Joint Chiefs of Staff.

Then, she served as George H.W. Bush’s senior director of Soviet and East European affairs in the National Security Council and as his special assistant for national security affairs.

Eventually, Rice was named as National Security Advisor in 2000. Five years later, Bush nominated Rice to be Secretary of State, a notion that was passed by a Senate vote.

After leaving office, Rice joined the College Football Playoff selection committee in Oct. 2013.

Notably, the former Secretary of State has penned nine published works throughout the years.

According to Celebrity Net Worth, Rice was making $305 thousand annually at Stanford University. Also, she raked in $405 thousand in 2019 for serving on the board of Dropbox.

Condoleezza Rice Instagram and Twitter

Currently, the politician possesses about 89 thousand Instagram followers. To go along with that, Rice has another 280 thousand followers on her Twitter account as of July 2022.

Personal Life

Interestingly, Rice co-owned a home in Palo Alto, California with Randy Bean from 2003 to 2017. Glenn Klesser revealed the living situation in his book, The Confidante: Condoleezza Rice and the Creation of the Bush Legacy, sparking rumors about the relationship.

Rice practices playing the piano to this day, even performing with a chamber music group.

Lastly, she owns honorary degrees from universities like Southern Methodist University, the University of Alabama, the University of Louisville, Boston College, and several others.

Condoleezza Rice Net Worth

The Condoleezza Rice net worth figure of $10 million has been amassed since the early 2000s.

Over that span, the former Secretary of State has yielded highly-profitable salaries from her jobs in the White House, at universities, and by serving on the board of several companies.

Condoleezza Rice FAQ

Who is Condoleezza Rice’s husband?

Actually, Rice has never been married and she has no children. Yet, she was once engaged to football player Rick Upchurch in the 1970s.

Who is Condoleezza Rice voting for?

While it’s not known who Rice voted for in the previous elections, we are aware that George W. Bush voted for Rice to be President of the United States in 2020.
