Britney Spears Son Spotted With Girlfriend In Hawaii As He Refuses To See His Mom

August 2024 · 3 minute read

Highlights

  • Britney Spears' eldest son, Jayden James Federline, was seen with a girlfriend in Hawaii, where the family now resides.
  • Britney didn't object to her sons moving away to Hawaii because she hadn't seen them in over a year.
  • Britney's sons have been refusing to see her due to her provocative social media content.

Britney Spears’ boys are all grown up, and it appears her eldest son even has a girlfriend.

Jayden James Federline, 17, who Britney shares with her ex-husband Kevin Federline, was seen getting close to a young woman in Hawaii on Monday, photos published by TMZ show. It was a family affair, as the teen was joined by his younger brother, Sean Preston, 16, his father, step-mother, and younger half-siblings.

Britney’s Son Has A Girlfriend Now That He’s Living In Hawaii

The whole family was dressed in beach attire for the sunny affair. Jayden was photographed in swimming trunks with a blue and white pattern, while his female friend wore a black two-piece swimsuit.

Britney’s sons relocated to Hawaii last fall with their father after his wife was offered a job in the tropical state. Britney didn’t challenge Kevin’s request, event though they share custody.

Why Britney Didn’t Object To Her Sons Moving Away

In May, it was first reported that the Federline family was hoping to move. Sources at the time told TMZ that while Britney didn’t like the idea of her boys so far away, she didn’t challenge it since she hadn’t seen them in over a year at that point.

“Britney hasn't seen the kids in more than a year and the kids want to leave town, it's likely the judge will allow the move,” an insider said.

Britney’s sons have apparently been refusing to see their famous mother, largely due to the provocative content she’s become known for sharing on socials.

“I think Mom has struggled giving us both attention and showing us equal love; I don’t think she showed enough to Preston, and I feel really bad for that,” Sean said in an interview to Daily Mail in 2022, explaining his strained relationship with Britney.

Kevin added to the outlet, “I try to explain to them, ‘Look, maybe that’s just another way she tries to express herself.’ But that doesn’t take away from the fact of what it does to them. It’s tough. I can’t imagine how it feels to be a teenager having to go to high school.”

Last month, Britney opened up about how difficult it’s been being isolated from their kids, which she claims started years ago after being placed in a conservatorship (which was lifted in 2021).

Posting on Instagram for her 42 million followers, Britney shared an old paparazzi photo showing her visiting one of her boys at his school during lunch. The singer says she could sometimes only see her sons by coming to their school. “I didn’t get to see my kids as much as I wanted so I would go to their school and have lunch with them!!! He just wanted me to hold him though !!! Our professional conversations,” she wrote.

Britney hasn’t commented on the new photos of Jayden and his friend. She’s continuing to navigate her ongoing divorce from Sam Asghari.
