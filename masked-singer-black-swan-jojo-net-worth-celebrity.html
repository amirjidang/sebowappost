The Poorest Celebrity Contestants On 'The Masked Singer' Ranked

August 2024 · 6 minute read

The Masked Singer has been on for six incredible seasons and has been able to get big-name stars to join the cast to compete for the trophy. Celebrities decide to go on The Masked Singer for many reasons. Some do it for the experience and to have fun. Others do it for a family member or for their kids at home. Other stars who may have been involved in controversy or scandals come on the show to be seen differently. Singers looking to change up their sound or work on making a comeback may come on The Masked Singer to begin that journey.

While many of the performers who come on The Masked Singer are considered the richest contestants according to their net worths. Comedian Wayne Brady for example was on season two of The Masked Singer as the Fox and ended up winning his season and has a net worth of $12 million. Talk show host Wendy Williams also competed on The Masked Singer as the Lips on season four and has a net worth of $40 million. Today, we are breaking down the poorest celebrities who have competed on The Masked Singer.

7 JoJo Is Worth $7 Million

Starting off the list is JoJo who was revealed as the lovely Black Swan in season five of The Masked Singer and ended up being in second place as the runner-up to Nick Lachey who was the adorable Piglet. JoJo is a talented singer-songwriter and actress who got her big break starring in the 2006 Disney Channel movie Aquamarine. She played the role of Hailey, who discovers the mermaid Aqua (played by Sara Paxton) with her friend Claire (played by Emma Roberts). Since then, JoJo released her first self-titled studio album in 2016 and has released 5 other albums since then with her latest album in 2021 titled Trying Not To Think About It. One thing JoJo has said she that she will take away from the experience of being on The Masked Singer and becoming the Black Swan changed her life.

6 Joey Fatone Is Worth $7 Million

Joey Fatone competed on The Masked Singer under the persona of the Rabbit in season one before getting eliminated in episode eight. Joey is best known for being a member of the popular boy band at the time NSYNC singing as the baritone. Joey even competed on the hit celebrity dance competition show Dancing With The Stars in 2007, so competing against other celebrities was nothing new to him. These days, Joey is the new host for the popular game show Family Feud since taking over from Steve Harvey.

5 Jordyn Woods Is Worth $6 Million

Jordyn Woods competed as the Kangaroo in season three of The Masked Singer before getting eliminated in episode 11. Jordyn is known for being best friends with Keeping Up With The Kardashians star Kylie Jenner before allegedly cheating with basketball player Tristan Thompson who was in a relationship with Kylie's half-sister Khloe Kardashian at the time. Much like her ex-best friend Kylie and her sister Kendall Jenner, Jordyn is a model as well. She had modelled for Khloe's clothing company Good American and for Kylie's makeup line Kylie's Cosmetics. However, since the controversy with Kylie and her family, Jordyn has moved on and is now a model for Torrid's new brand Lovesick.

4 Tori Kelly Is Worth $5 Million

Tori Kelly competed as the Seahorse in season four of The Masked Singer before getting eliminated in episode 10 and is not the only singing competition show she has competed on. Tori got her start as a singer competing in season 9 of American Idol back in 2010 but did not make it to the top 24 despite her best efforts. Although she has had quite a successful career considering shortly after being cut off on American Idol early she was swooped up to be a Battle Advisor for Team Adam Levine in Season 10 of The Voice. More recently Tori has voiced the main character role of Meena in the 2016 Disney animated film Sing and in its 2021 sequel.

3 Adrienne Bailon Is Worth $4 million

Adrienne Bailon competed as the beautiful Flamingo on The Masked Singer and her musical past helped her land in the season two finale getting third place overall. Many fans of the reality television show would likely recognize Adrienne from her role as Chanel in the popular Disney film The Cheetah Girls. Adrienne is not the only Cheetah Girl to compete on The Masked Singer because her co-star and close friend Raven Symone also competed on the show in the same season as The Black Widow. Adrienne is also rumoured to be reuniting again soon with Raven in Raven's new season of That's So Raven's spin-off series Raven's Home, where Adrienne will reprise her role as Raven's former high school Alana.

2 Bobby Brown Is Worth $2 Million

Bobby Brown competed as the Crab in season five of The Masked Singer before getting eliminated in episode seven.

Bobby Brown is a well-known rapper for many generations however he originally started out in his R&B group New Edition in 1978 before they went their separate ways in 1985 which is when Bobby decided to begin his solo journey as a rapper. Bobby Brown decided to join the season 5 cast of The Masked Singer, so viewers can see a different side of him. Bobby is also hopeful it will help him make a comeback into his career after he was put on hold due to some mental health, substance abuse and legal troubles Bobby went through. When he was eliminated from The Masked Singer, Bobby Brown said the experience has changed his life ultimately for the better.

1 Honey Boo Boo Is Worth $1 Million

Last on the list is reality television star Honey Boo Boo who competed with her Mama June as the Beach Ball coming in on the eighth episode of season six as the shows Wild Card. Fans had a lot to say about their performance After their time was cut short getting eliminated that same episode. Honey Boo Boo grew up in the spotlight in her television series Here Comes Honey Boo Boo from 2012 to 2014. Honey Boo Boo is now 16 years old and still in the reality tv spotlight starring in her series spinoff Mama June: From Not To Hot.
