Everything We Know About Jennifer Aniston And Ellen DeGeneres' Friendship Timeline

August 2024 Â· 6 minute read

Ellen DeGeneres and Jennifer Aniston have both been major figures in Hollywood since the 1980s. The two have had their success on a number of projects including Aniston's hit sitcom 'Friends', and Ellen's long-lasting talk show 'The Ellen DeGeneres Show'. While Hollywood can appear to be a massive entity, it is quite a small world, as both Ellen and Jen have become the best of friends.

Their friendship first began in the mid-90s and has blossomed into one of our favourite celebrity friendships to date. Not only has Aniston appeared on 'The Ellen Show' more times than we can count, but she and Ellen have formed a bond unlike any other we have ever seen.

The two have worked together, lived by one another, and have supported each other through the ups and downs of both their lives and careers. If there is one friendship that we can get behind, it is definitely Ellen and Jennifer's. If you aren't sure just how close these two, here is a timeline of 15 photos showing Ellen and Aniston's close friendship.

15 Film Premiere - 1998

Jennifer Aniston and Ellen DeGeneres have definitely been friends for much longer than we could have ever imagined. The two have been kicking it back since the late 90s, and were even photographed together at Jennifer Aniston's 1998 movie premiere for 'The Object Of My Affection'.

14 Ellen's First-Ever Guest - 2003

While we know the two have built quite the loving bond, many fans of both Jennifer Aniston and Ellen DeGeneres might not know that Jennifer was actually Ellen's first-ever guest on her talk show! The 'Ellen Show' premiered way back in 2003, and Aniston made history as being the first-ever guest to appear on the show.

13 Makeup Meltdown - 2012

Over the course of the next decade, Ellen and Jennifer would become very close! The two have spoken openly about living very close to one another, and have supported each other through it all. Flashforward to 2012, and it appears they've gotten so close that Jennifer Aniston felt comfortable enough to let her friend Ellen play around with some makeup during a sketch for 'The Ellen Show'.

12 People's Choice Awards - 2013

There was no denying that these two were the best of friends! Not only is Jennifer Aniston close with Ellen, but in turn, she has also developed a very close relationship with Ellen's wife and fellow actress, Portia De Rossi. The three of them were spotted posing backstage at the 2013 People's Choice Awards, in which they have all successfully won numerous awards!

11 'FRIENDS' Reunion - 2013

As the two began to get closer and closer, it was really up to Ellen to get what everyone had been waiting for, a 'Friends' reunion. In 2013, Ellen was one of the first people to get up to three cast members from the iconic show, to act in a hilarious sketch! Jennifer starred alongside former castmates Courtney Cox and Matthew Perry in what will probably be the closest thing we will get to a 'Friends' reunion ever.

10 Fun For The Whole Family - 2014

Jennifer Aniston has appeared on 'The Ellen Show' more times than we can remember, so much that she even guest-hosted twice! With that being said, Ellen's show is a place for the whole family, and fans were delighted back in 2014 when Jennifer Aniston appeared with her famous dad, John Aniston, on the talk show.

9 Wedding Bells - 2014

In 2014, it was revealed that Jennifer Aniston and Justin Theroux were engaged to be married! This was major news at the times, and one person that revelled in excitement was none other than Ellen. Jennifer paid Ellen a visit on her show, where the two gabbed about wedding planning and details surrounding the magical day.

8 Sumo-ing It Up - 2016

Jennifer and Ellen share quite the special bond, so much that the two can be extremely silly with one another, so much that they even went as far as dressing up in inflatable sumo wrestler costumes for a segment on 'The Ellen Show' back in 2016. It was at this moment that is was very clear that Aniston was a ride or die kind of friend.

7 Endless Support System - 2017

Fans and viewers worldwide could see just how special of a friendship these two shared. It was revealed that when Jennifer Aniston's private plane had to make an emergency landing, Ellen was the first person to send her a text to make sure everything was alright. The moment was quite scary, Aniston recalls, and she was delighted to see that her good friend Ellen was the first to reach out.

6 The Tres Amigas - 2018

While we are certainly jealous of Ellen and Jennifer's friendship, we have gotten even more jealous knowing that the two are also very close with former first lady, Michelle Obama! Talk about a Tres Amigas situation. If we could be friends with any group out there, it would without a doubt in the world be this one.

5 Power Duo - 2018

Ellen DeGeneres and Jennifer Aniston have not only been by one another's side through the highs and lows, but they are both such rays of light that have provided so much positivity and good into the world. They say opposites attract, but it is very clear that Ellen and Jennifer are very much like-minded people who have done great things with their platforms.

4 Cooking Up A Storm - 2018

One of our favourite Ellen and Jennifer memories was back in 2018 when the two decided to spice things up on the 'Ellen Show' stage alongside Ayesha Curry. With Curry's cookbook having just been released, it was only fitting to have Ellen and Jennifer cook with her and judging by the episode, it looks like they had the best time.

3 The Bestfriend Brawl - 2019

Ellen can be very territorial, and rightfully so, however, when Jennifer Aniston and Reese Witherspoon began working on their 'Morning Show' project together, Ellen got a little bit jealous. During Reese's visit to the 'Ellen Show' last year, she and Ellen had a back and forth over who was better friends with Aniston. After Witherspoon left the stage, Ellen phoned Jennifer to ensure that she was her best friend, in which Aniston confirmed with a whopping yes. Sorry, Reese!

2 Golden Globes - 2020

We first saw Ellen and Jennifer back in 1998 at Aniston's movie premiere, and here we are 22 years later, and the two are closer than ever. They were spotted posing together at the 2020 Golden Globes alongside Charlize Theron, and it is definitely heartwarming to see that their friendship has come such a long way.

1 Always There To Help - 2020

To wrap things up, we wanted to show just how supportive these two are of one another. The 2020 Golden Globes saw many memorable moments, however, our favourite is none other than Ellen being the good friend that she is! As pictures, Ellen is holding Aniston's dress up for her so that she can walk to their table easily. Talk about #FriendshipGoals.
