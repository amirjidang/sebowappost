Sam Riegel Biography

Sam Riegel is an American voice actor, director, and writer born in Washington DC. He is famously known for starring in Critical Role in which he and other fellow voice actors play Dungeons & Dragons.

Sam is also known for his voice roles as Donatello in the 2003 Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles series and Phoenix Wright in the Ace Attorney games. he was also a voice director for Fresh Beat Band of Spies and Sanjay and Craig for Nickelodeon. He also voiced Riven in the Nickelodeon revival of Winx Club.

Sam Riegel Family

Sam was born in Washington, D.C to Kurt Riegel and Lenore Riegel. His father, Kurt, is a radio astronomer, environmental executive, and sailor. His mother later got married to Jerome Charyn, an author. Sam is of Jewish descent. His grandmother was a Holocaust survivor. Sam has two siblings: his sister Eden Riegel and his half-brother Tatiana S. Riegel. His sister is an actress while his brother is a filmmaker.

Sam Riegel Wife

Sam is married to Quyen Tran. Together, the couple has been blessed with a daughter and a son. Sam resides in Los Angeles with his family. On September 11, 2001, Sam and his mother, Tran witnessed the 9/11 attacks.
