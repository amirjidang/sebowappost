The Real Reason Meghann Fahy And Leo Woodall Keep Their Alleged Relationship A Secret After Meeting

August 2024 · 5 minute read

It is no secret that when actors work closely together, sometimes the boundaries of their professional relationship become blurred. There have been multiple instances where co-stars either formed a great friendship or started dating. And fans are convinced this happened between Meghann Fahy and Leo Woodall.

The two star on White Lotus Season 2; Fahy plays the character Daphne Sullivan while Woodall plays the role of Jack. But why have they kept their relationship so secret if they are dating? Is there something these two stars don't want fans to know? Are they guarding a secret? Or is the situation more simple to explain?

When Did The Meghann Fahy And Leo Woodall Dating Rumors Start?

The Meghann Fahy and Leo Woodall dating rumors have been around since late 2022 and are not slowing down. There have been multiple instances where co-stars formed a great friendship or started dating (including Kaley Cuoco and Johnny Galecki, Lili Reinhart, and Cole Sprouse, and Miley Cyrus and Lucas Till, to name a few). But despite being asked in multiple interviews what is going on, the alleged couple have dodged the questions or given cryptic answers (which, in a way, neither confirms nor denies what is going on between them).

For example, instead of just saying, "no, nothing is happening at all," Fahy answered a question about her dating Woodall with the response: "I don't kiss and tell." In the same interview on "Watch What Happens Live," host Andy Cohen asked if they were "friends with benefits," to which Fahy replied: "We're friends." For most people, this last comment would be enough to accept that the rumors are false and move on, but that has not slowed the speculation.

But what else started the speculation? That could have something to do with Instagram, and the co-stars have posted images together and written what many people interpret as affection comments (although they can also be completely platonic).

In a post, Fahy allegedly commented: "I love you! I love these! I love you!" to which Woodhall responded: "Love you right back." But this is hardly definitive proof, as much as many of us would like it to be.

Plus, they need to be together to promote the show, so it only further fuels speculation when people see them, for example, on the red carpet at the 2023 Screen Actors Guild Awards.

Why Would Meghann Fahy And Leo Woodall Want To Keep Their Relationship A Secret?

There is no definitive proof as to why Meghann Fahy and Leo Woodall would want to keep their alleged relationship a secret if they were dating, but there are some things to consider which make sense. First, actors dating each other after starring in a show is nothing new, but it can also be bad for business.

This is especially true if the stars break up while filming, and it can create awkward tension and a lack of chemistry. If the whole world is not focusing on the relationship, and it is not under a microscope, it can be much easier to forget about the whole thing and move on. It would be much harder having to read headlines about yourself and your former partner if you were heartbroken.

Another reason could be the considerable age gap. Meghann Fahy was born on April 25, 1990, while Leo Woodhall is seven years her junior and was born on September 14, 1996. While seven years doesn't seem like that much later in life, it is entirely possible that they are in completely different phases right now, and it would be hard to maintain their relationship (if they are in one).

Additionally, it's clear the couple wants to keep their private life private because it's no one's business but their own. We have witnessed all too many times the pressure that being in public has had on celebrity relationships, and this intensifies when they have come forward and confirmed they were an item.

Inside Meghann Fahy And Leo Woodall's Careers

Meghann Fahy and Leo Woodall are up-and-coming stars who we expect to see much more of in the future. This could be another reason they don't want romance reports overshadowing their accomplishments. And they are certainly accomplishing a lot.

In an interview with Harper's Bazaar in 2022, Fahy spoke about her gratitude for landing a role as she did in White Lotus 2. "I never planned to be an actor. So every single thing that comes my way is just something that I feel really lucky to have the opportunity to do," she said.

"I'm excited to see what's next. I've always been really into the actors that are in everything, but nobody really knows who they are. So you'll see them at the grocery store, and you're like, 'That's the girl from the... wait, what did we see her in?' I don't have big dreams of winning fancy awards or being a famous person. I just love making things."

What else can we gather from their interviews? Truthfully, not much. Meghann Fahy was asked about people she bonded with while filming White Lotus 2 in an interview with Glamour, and her response did mention Leo Woodhall, but she called him her friend. Interesting, isn't it?

Here is what she said: "I made a few friends! Which, I always say, if you just meet one person that you really click with, that's a win, but yeah, a few people that I'm really close with. Leo Woodall and I are really good friends; Adam DiMarco; we see Haley [Lu Richardson] all the time. Aubrey [Plaza] 's super busy, so I don't see her very often. I just truly love every single person that I got to work with…" It is entirely possible that these two stars are exactly what they say they are: friends!
