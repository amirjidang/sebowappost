What's The Cast Of Baby Daddy Doing Today?

August 2024 Â· 5 minute read

The People's Choice Award-winning comedy series on Freeform, formerly known as ABCFamily, Baby Daddy, ended after six successful seasons in 2017. The show centered on a young, single dad and his friends and family who help him to raise his little girl. The series starred Jean-Luc Bilodeau, Derek Theler, Melissa Peterman, Chelsea Kane, Tahj Mowry, and Peter Porte.

Since the show ended, the cast has gone on to do various different types of projects, from working with the Muppets, to performing on stage at the Hollywood Bowl, to doing voiceover work. The cast has been keeping themselves busy over the last several years, including making it through a global pandemic. Let's take a deep dive into what the cast of Baby Daddy has been up to since the show ended in 2017.

8 Jean-Luc Bilodeau Was In A CBS Sitcom

Jean-Luc Bilodeau was a series regular on the CBS comedy series, Carol's Second Act, which ran for one 18-episode season. He portrayed the role of Dr. Daniel Kutcher. He is currently slated to be in the film Marbles, which stars Matt Dallas and Jama Williamson.

7 Bilodeau Is Doing More Than Just Acting

Other than that, the actor has been enjoying traveling the world with his current girlfriend. He has been to Thailand, Costa Rica, and Mexico, and has also spent time in various states across America and went to the 2022 Coachella music festival. He has also been spending a lot of time at home in Canada, where he is from.

6 Derek Theler Has Been Appearing In Guest Roles On TV

Since Baby Daddy finished it's run in 2017, series star Derek Theler has gone on to guest roles in television series such as Dollface, American Gods, Wayne, and American Housewife. He also appeared in six episodes of the series Ninjak vs the Valiant Universe. Most recently, he had a series regular role on the Paramount Network series, 68 Whiskey, which was canceled after one season in 2020. Theler also spends as much time as he can with his two nephews who live in Colorado and does what he can to raise awareness of Type 1 diabetes, which he has.

5 Melissa Peterman Was On Young Sheldon

Melissa Peterman has done some episodes of Last Man Standing and Sydney to the Max since her Baby Daddy days came to an end. She had a recurring role on Young Sheldon playing the role of Brenda Sparks in 26 episodes of the series. She has also been a part of the yearly Sound of Music sing-a-long at the Hollywood Bowl for many years now. Most recently, she provided the voice for the character Starla Darling in the animated movie My Babysitter the Super Hero. She sees her onscreen love interest, Peter Porte as often as she can and gets together with her two onscreen children when she can, as well. Bilodeau and Theler hung out with her in November 2021 and posted about it on social media.

4 Chelsea Kane Has Been Doing Voiceover Work

Chelsea Kane, who played Riley on Baby Daddy, has been doing a ton of voice work since the show ended. She did a voice in an episode of Trolls: The Beat Goes On! in 2018 as well as a voice in an episode of Robot Chicken in 2019. She was a series regular in the series Hot Streets as the voice of Jen Sanders and was also a regular on the series Archibald's Next Big Thing, voicing the character of Loy. In 2020, Kane voiced the character of Rose Wilson in an episode of the animated series DC Super Hero Girls.

3 But She Hasn't Stopped Working In Front Of The Camera

While she has mostly transitioned to voice work, Kane has had one role in front of the camera since Baby Daddy came to an end. She had a four-episode stint on The Expanding Universe of Ashley Garcia in 2020, playing the role of Ava Germaine.

2 Tahj Mowry Is Working With The Muppets

Tahj Mowry, known as the younger brother of Sister, Sister twins, Tia and Tamera Mowry, and for playing the role of the hilarious Tucker on Baby Daddy, is currently working on filming a new series with the Muppets, titled The Muppets Mayhem. He also recently worked on a film called Me Time starring Mark Wahlberg, Kevin Hart and Regina Hall. He was a regular in a short-lived television series called How We Roll in 2022 and made an appearance in an episode of the Netflix series Family Reunion in 2021.

1 Peter Porte Has Done A Ton Of TV Movies

Peter Porte has filmed a ton of movies for TV since Baby Daddy ended. Since 2017, he has appeared in Love at the Shore, A Gift for Christmas, Love, Once and Always, Christmas Harmony, Rome in Love, Cherished Memories: A Gift to Remember 2, Dashing in December, and Dead in the Water. He has also appeared in four episodes of Days of Our Lives: Beyond Salem, which streams on Peacock. Most recently, he appeared in two episodes of the Amazon Prime series, With Love.
