A Peek Inside PSG Star Neymar Jr.s Multi-Million-Dollar Garage

Neymar da Silva Santos Júnior, aka Neymar, is one of the most famous footballers in the world, known for his skills on the field and his flamboyant lifestyle off it. The Brazilian superstar has accumulated a massive $200 Million net worth over a supremely lucrative football career. He is known to be an avid car enthusiast and has built up an impressive collection of luxury vehicles over the years.

Neymar’s car collection includes some of the rarest and most exotic cars in the world worth millions of dollars. He has everything from Ferrari to Maserati, and his garage is any gearhead’s dream.

Mercedes-AMG GT - $118,600

Neymar's Mercedes-AMG GT is an exceptional sports car combining breathtaking performance and luxurious refinement. Its powerful 4.0-liter V8 twin-turbo engine produces an impressive 469 horses and 465 lb-ft of torque, allowing the car to go from 0 to 60 miles per hour in just 3.9 seconds. The car's top speed is an incredible 189 miles per hour, thanks to its advanced aerodynamics and lightweight construction. The Mercedes-AMG GT also features a 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission providing smooth, lightning-fast gear changes.

Audi RS7 - $123,900

Neymar’s multi-million dollar garage includes an exotic Audi RS7. This high-performance luxury sedan is a perfect fit for the football superstar. The Audi RS7 has a 4.0-liter V8 engine that produces 560 horsepower, allowing it to go from 0 to 60 miles per hour in just 3.7 seconds. The car's top speed is an impressive 190 miles per hour, making it one of the fastest cars in Neymar's collection. Its exterior is sleek and elegant, with a matt gray finish that will turn heads, as per Hot Cars.

Audi R8 Spyder V10 Plus - $171,000

Another stunning car in Neymar's impressive collection is the Audi R8 Spyder V10 Plus, a convertible sports car that captures the thrill and excitement of high-performance driving. The car is powered by a 5.2-liter V10 engine that delivers an impressive 610 horsepower and 413 lb-ft of torque, allowing it to go from 0 to 60 miles per hour in just 3.3 seconds. Its top speed is an impressive 203 miles per hour, making it one of the fastest convertible cars on the market.

Ferrari GTC4Lusso - $263,750

The Ferrari GTC4Lusso is an exceptional grand touring car that is both practical and powerful. It features a 6.3-liter V12 engine that produces an impressive 680 horsepower and 514 lb-ft of torque, making it one of the most potent cars in Neymar's collection. The car's unique all-wheel drive system ensures optimal traction and handling, even in challenging driving conditions. Neymar has been spotted zooming around with his GTC4Lusso in the streets of Paris.

Ferrari 458 Italia - $279,687

The second Ferrari in Neymar’s expensive car collection, The Ferrari 458 Italia, is a work of art. The 458 Italia is a stunning supercar that perfectly embodies the Italian automaker's high performance and style heritage. Its 4.5-liter V8 engine produces an impressive 570 horsepower and also features a 7-speed dual-clutch transmission that provides lightning-fast gear changes and a smooth and precise driving experience. The car's advanced aerodynamics and lightweight construction ensure optimal performance and handling, while its sleek and angular design turns heads wherever it goes.

Maserati MC12 - $1.5 Million

Priced at a whopping $1.5 Million, the Maserati MC12 is one of Neymar's garage's most expensive and rarest cars. The MC12 was built to celebrate Maserati's victory in the FIA GT championship. As per Motor Trend, the MC12 is powered by a massive 6.0-liter V12 engine that produces an astonishing 624 horsepower and 479 lb-ft of torque, allowing the car to reach a top speed of over 200 miles per hour. Its aerodynamic design, with a low-slung body and a prominent rear spoiler, ensures optimal performance and handling at high speeds. The car's exclusive limited production run of only 50 units makes it an extremely rare and valuable addition to any car collection.

Lykan Hypersport - $3.4 Million

Neymar's collection also includes a $3.4 Million Lykan Hypersport, one of the world's most expensive and exclusive cars. Only seven units of this supercar were produced, making it a rare and valuable addition to any collection. The car features a 3.7-liter twin-turbocharged flat-six engine that delivers a staggering 780 horses, allowing it to go from 0 to 60 MPH in mere 2.8 seconds, with a top speed of over 240 MPH as per New Atlas. The Lykan Hypersport also features high-tech features, including a holographic display system and a diamond-encrusted LED headlight system.

Lamborghini Veneno - $4 Million

An exquisite Lamborghini Veneno graces Neymar’s spectacular garage. The Veneno is one of the rarest and costliest cars on the planet, with a mind-boggling $4 Million price tag. Sports Brief reported that it was launched to celebrate Lamborghini’s 50th Anniversary at the 2013 Geneva Motor Show. This supercar is powered by a 6.5-liter V12 engine that delivers an astonishing 750 horsepower, allowing the car to go from 0 to 60 MPH in just 2.8 seconds, with a top speed of over 220 MPH. The vehicle features a range of high-tech features, including an innovative aerodynamic system that automatically adjusts to the driving conditions for maximum performance. Only three units of this exotic Lambo have been manufactured to date.

Neymar's car collection reflects not just his love for luxury and his passion for cars but also his success as a footballer. As one of the highest-paid athletes in the world, Neymar's car collection symbolizes his hard work and dedication to his craft. Following his gearhead spirit, he has made himself one of the most exclusive garages in the world, lined up with rare and expensive supercars. The Lamborghini Veneno stands out as the most expensive car in Neymar’s garage, with a $4 Million price tag.
