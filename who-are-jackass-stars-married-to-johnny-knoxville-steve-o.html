Who Are The Jackass Stars Married To?

August 2024 · 5 minute read

When your job involves smashing your groin with hammers, shoving inanimate objects into certain body cavities, or surviving raging bulls, one might think that finding true love would be an impossible challenge. One would also think that with that kind of trauma to the groin, children wouldn’t be an option for them. Evidently, love always finds a way and medical science has made strides in groin-related trauma treatments. How else can one explain how the stars of Jackass not only have wives and girlfriends, but some have multiple children as well?

Related: How Steve-O Made Almost No Money For The Entire First Season Of Jackass

If anyone is going to make a living doing the kind of stunts that the Jackass boys do, then any partner they have would need to be a very understanding and supportive one. It can’t be easy to watch your partner beat the living snot out of themselves for fun (or for a living). But, as we said, love finds a way. Jackass superstar Steve-O recently got engaged, Wee Man has been spotted around town with his latest girlfriend, Preston Lacy is single and ready to mingle, and the Jackass patriarch Johnny Knoxville has been happily married for several years now. Many of the original crew members have also been married once before their current partners. Let’s learn a little about the party-hardy stuntmen of Jackass and the women who love them.

Updated on September 7, 2022: While Jason Acuña remains unengaged as of now, it seems as though he is serious about his relationship with partner Cassandra Brett. She very rarely posts on social media, sharing only one post so far this year, but we see plenty of her on Wee Man's page. Just earlier this month, Acuña shared several photos of a trip to Hawaii, in which Cassandra came with him.

7 Steve-O Is Engaged To Hairstylist Lux Wright

Steve-O was once married to a woman named Britney McGraw, but the couple divorced back in 2008, around the same time that Steve-O entered rehab for his severe drug and alcohol problems. Now 13 years sober, Steve-O has found a new love in hairstylist Lux Wright. The couple announced their engagement on Instagram in 2019. The pair have admitted that while they are excited about marriage, they do not want or plan to have children.

Related: Did Steve-O And Johnny Knoxville Get Into An Argument Before The Shooting Of Jackass Forever?

6 Chris Pontius Married His Wife Mae In 2018

Chris Pontius, who hosts the show Wildboyz with his friend Steve-O, has also been married twice. Chris Pontius was married to the lovely Clair Nolan in 2004, and they divorced in 2013. He married his new wife Mae Quijada (now Mae Pontius) around 2018 in a private ceremony. Chris Pontius announced the birth of their first son on Instagram in 2019.

5 Dave England Has Two Children With His Wife, Shawna

Dave England, a.k.a. Darf (his drunken alter-ego), is happily married to a woman named Shawna England, and they have two children together: their sons Van England and Clyde England. Dave England also has two other children from a previous relationship, though he has only ever been married to Shawna.

4 Jason ‘Wee Man’ Acuña Is Dating A Woman Named Cassandra Brett

Jason “Wee Man” Acuña has been playing the field for a long time but might be ready to finally settle down. Although he is not yet married, he has been frequently spotted with his current partner Cassandra Brett around his home turf in Redondo Beach, CA. In 2006, Wee Man was expecting a child with his then-girlfriend (whose name was never made public), but the couple tragically lost the baby.

3 Erhen McGhenhey Keeps His Dating Life Extremely Private

Erhen McGhenhey, a.k.a. Danger Erhen, is one of the most private members of the Jackass crew. Since breaking his neck in a snowboarding accident he has kept his stunt work to a minimum, but is still an active part of the crew, although he is often the butt of many cruel jokes. Erhen, who is now 45 years old, has not made it clear to the public whether he is single, engaged, or married.

2 Preston Lacy Is Extremely Single

We’ll keep this one short; Preston Lacy is very much single and has never been married or engaged. There is no report of him dating anyone at the moment. Preston Lacy is 52 years old.

1 Johnny Knoxville Has Been Happily Married To Naomi Nelson For Years

Knoxville, like several of his other co-stars, is divorced. He was married to his first wife the longest out of all of his friends who were also married one time before. Knoxville was with his first wife, Melanie Cates, from 1995 to 2009, and allegedly they broke up over Johnny Knoxville's relationship with his Dukes of Hazzard co-star,Jessica Simpson. Soon after the divorce, he married his next girlfriend Naomi Nelson after she gave birth to their first child in 2009. Some believe that it is because of his relationship with his wife and children that Knoxville has finally decided to retire from stunts, but not without his final hoorah with his pals in Jackass Forever. The married couple now have three children together.
