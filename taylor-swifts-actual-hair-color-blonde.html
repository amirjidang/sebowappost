Taylor Swift's Hairstyles Have Evolved Over Her Career

Taylor Swift has changed her hair color dramatically for performances on-stage and music videos. She can surprise with style, nearly unrecognizable with a dark hair color. Other times she stuns with a much brighter look than her usual sunshine shade. At times, the star has made bold moves with her own hair color.

As Swift transitioned to a pop star, her style began to change. Around 2010, she appeared with a darker shade of blonde, and her coils had straightened. The Cruel Summer singer could often be seen wearing bright red lipstick, now one of her signature looks. After her copper color, in 2017 she made a switch to platinum blonde, but it seems Swift thought the color was a miss, according to ShowBizCheatSheet.

"We very much wanted to give Taylor a new look. And make her into a sort of rock n' roll chick," Anna Wintour, who reportedly suggested the hairstyle, told Today. Swift paired her bleached hair with incredibly dark lipstick for the 2016 Met Gala, and stayed platinum blonde for Coachella.

The Shake It Off singer told Elle how she felt about the color. "Fashion is all about playful experimentation. If you don't look back at pictures of some of your old looks and cringe, you're doing it wrong. See: Bleachella."

Her blonde hair has been golden, copper, ash blonde, platinum and Swift has reportedly highlighted and layered her blonde hair to provide depth and texture to her luscious locks. She's also experimented with pastel colors, including pink and blue at different times amid her golden waves. Swift has gone back to her lengthy golden locks in recent years, and still impresses many with changes she makes for videos and performances.
