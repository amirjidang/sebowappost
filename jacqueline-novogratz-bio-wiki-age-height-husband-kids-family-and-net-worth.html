    Jacqueline Novogratz CEO

    Novogratz started her profession at Chase Manhattan Bank in 1983, as an international credit analyst. After three years, she dropped banking to search for how to make a difference in society.

    She worked throughout Africa as a specialist for the World Bank and for UNICEF. As a UNICEF consultant in Rwanda in the late 1980s, she helped found Duterimbere, Rwanda’s first microfinance institution. Novogratz established and directed The Philanthropy Workshop and The Next Generation Leadership programs at the Rockefeller Foundation before establishing Acumen in 2001.
