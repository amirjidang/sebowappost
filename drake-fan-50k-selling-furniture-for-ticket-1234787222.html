Drake Gifts Fan $50K After Selling His Furniture For A Ticket To Show

August 2024 · 2 minute read

Drake has continued his streak of charitable gestures while on his Its All A Blur Tour. During his recent Las Vegas show, Drizzy gifted a fan $50,000 after learning that the attendee spent furniture money on two tickets to two separate shows.

On Sunday (Sept. 3), The Shade Room caught a clip of the 6 God reading the fan’s sign before telling him he’d reimburse him with more. Drake then spoke about having empathy for others.

“You know what my man. Your furniture money, I’mma give you 50 bands tonight ’cause I love you,” he told the fan. “You never know what the person in front of you, left of you, right of you, behind you, is going through.”

At another show date last month, the Grammy-winner gifted a pink Birkin bag to a lucky fan in the front row.

“Drake ain’t cheap,” he said about the $30,000 bag. He then pointed to the fan to give it to before adding, “Give this to her right here. Give her that nice little Birkin bag. Give that girl that nice little Birkin, take that home with you. Make sure she has security on the way out, too, so nobody robs her for it.”
