Cameron Robbie- All About Margot Robbie Brother: Family And Siblings

August 2024 · 4 minute read

Cameron Robbie is a model, actor, and presenter from Australia. He is the younger brother of Margot Robbie, a two-time Oscar contender. Aside from being regarded as the Hollywood star brother, Cameron has established himself in the Australian film business with his own abilities and desires. Let’s find out more about Margot’s younger brother, Cameron Robbie, including his hobbies, acting profession, net worth, and connection with Margot.

Cameron Robbie Is a Native of Australia

Cameron was born in Dalby, Queensland, Australia on February 9, 1995. Cameron, 28, is of Australian nationality and of mixed ancestry of Scottish-German origin.

Education

Cameron went to Somerset College on the Gold Coast and participated in theater and music. Similarly, he holds a Bond University degree in cinema and television. In addition, he participated in school cricket and rugby.

Who Are Cameron Robbie’s Parents?

Robbie is the youngest of four children born to physiotherapist Sarie Kessler and former farm owner and property developer Dough Robbie. Dough, Cameron’s father, abandoned the family when all of the children were quite small.

His Mother Raised Him and His Siblings on Her Own.

Sarie Kessler raised all four children alone on the family property on Australia’s coast when Dough left the family. Cameron’s grandpa owned the property. Kessler encouraged all of her children to pursue their ambitions.

How Many Siblings Does Cameron Have?

Anya Robbie, Lachlan Robbie, and Margot Robbie are Robbie’s three siblings. In their close-knit family, they all spent their childhood together.

Anya Robbie Remains Out of the Public Eye and the Film Industry

Anya is the oldest of the group. She graduated from Bond University and works as a senior accountant at Accounts Pty. Ltd. She prefers to live a solitary life, therefore nothing is known about her personal life.

Lachlan Robbie: The elder brother

Lachlan, often known as Lockie, was born in 1989. He is well-known as a notable Hollywood stuntman who performed as a stuntman in various films, most notably Aquaman (2018) and Alien: Covenant (2017). He has also collaborated with his sister Margot Robbie on the film Birds of Prey.

Margot Robbie Is the Most Well-Known of Her Siblings

Robbie (born July 2, 1990) is a Somerset College graduate most known for her portrayal as ‘Harley Quinn’ in Suicide Squad (2016). The two-time Oscar nominee found success in a variety of roles, ranging from TV serial opera Neighbours to blockbuster movie The Wolf of Wall Street. Furthermore, Margot founded LuckyChap Entertainment with her husband Tom Ackerley, whom she married in 2016. Cameron began working as a crew member in his sister’s production firm as well.

Cameron has a closer relationship with Margot than with the other two. This might be because Cameron and Margot have always had a penchant for performing. Margot, the actress and producer, is Cameron’s staunchest supporter, and he looks to her for professional guidance.

Film and television roles

Cameron began his acting career in 2015, when he participated in Gifted, a short film made by his sister Margot and her husband Tom. Concurrently, he appeared in the short film People You May Know (2016). In 2017, he made his broadcast debut in the Australian serial drama I.C.U.

Cameron was also a part of the LuckyChap Entertainment staff. He worked on films such as Terminal, Dreamand, and Promising Young Woman. He is also an accomplished guitarist and vocalist. Cameron is also the face of many companies. He is often seen advertising and modeling for various products on his Instagram profiles. He works with companies such as Unison Label, Dior, and Cupra.

Is Cameron Robbie  Dating Anyone?

Cameron Robbie’s dating status remains unknown. Cameron, like other celebrities, wishes to keep his relationship secret. However, his Instagram photos sometimes make followers wonder whether Cameron has a woman love. He was previously reported to be dating Chiara Charlotte, which has yet to be proven by facts.

Cameron Robbie’s Net worth

Cameron, like his brothers, began working at an early age. He is financially self-sufficient and can support himself. His estimated net worth is $1.5 million.
