Ever since she decided to leave Teen Mom 2, Chelsea Houska is living a brand-new life. While Chelsea has shared over the years that things were less than perfect between Aubree Sky Lind-DeBoer and Adam Lind, what is their relationship like now?

Does Aubree Sky Lind-DeBoer Get Along With Her Father Adam Lind?

Chelsea DeBoer left Teen Mom 2 because she wanted to make sure that Aubree Sky Lind-DeBoer would be happy. But while Chelsea has always been a caring and devoted parent, the same thing can't be said of Adam Lind. Fans of the Teen Mom franchise know that there have been some really tough relationships on these MTV shows. And Chelsea went through a lot with Adam.

In 2022, Chelsea DeBoer spoke on Instagram Live about whether Aubree Sky Lind-DeBoer saw her father Adam Lind. Chelsea suggested that she hadn't been in touch with him for a while and that she didn't know much about what was happening in his life.

According to In Touch Weekly, Chelsea said, “I think he’s doing pretty good. I don’t know because I don’t speak to him personally, but I have heard some good things, that he’s in a good place.”

In February 2023, Chelsea said something a bit more positive about Aubree's relationship with Adam. She explained to Us Weekly, “There’s no bad vibes and it’s just a very open relationship. There’s no bad feelings. We see him sometimes and it’s totally fine.”

It sounds like the relationship is better than it once was. However, the fact that Chelsea said that she and Aubree get together with Adam "sometimes" suggests that they don't spend time with him regularly.

Teen Mom 2 fans definitely remember how hard Chelsea and Adam's relationship was and how things got bad after she gave birth to Aubree. In season 3, the couple was still together, although they had broken up before and then reunited. Aubree was three years old at the time.

According to Us Weekly, in one Teen Mom 2 scene, Adam and Chelsea took Aubree to a restaurant, and when she was unhappy, he laughed instead of figuring out how to solve the problem.

Chelsea talked about her relationship with Adam in an interview with Radar Online back in 2011. At the time, she sounded hopeful that she and Adam would be able to work things out. She said, "It’s no secret that we have had our ups and downs, but we are doing our best to work through it. I think it’s been more difficult for him to give up being young and care free than it was for me, which is how it is for a lot of young fathers from what I've seen. I believe we are both still learning about life and being new parents like most people are with their first child. It’s a growing and learning experience."

That didn't end up happening. And in 2014, Chelsea and her now husband Cole DeBoer started their relationship.

Over the past few years, Adam Lind has a few photos of Aubree Sky-Lind DeBoer on his Instagram account. This suggests that she has been in his life at some point.

However, Chelsea and Adam's relationship has never been smooth. In 2017, news came out of Adam's arrest for "violation of conditional bond no contact in domestic arrest," according to E! News. The publication reported that this was because of a situation with Adam's ex Stasia Huber. In October 2022, Lind was arrested because he didn't pay child support, according to The Hollywood Gossip.
