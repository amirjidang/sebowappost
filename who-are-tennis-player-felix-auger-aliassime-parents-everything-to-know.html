Who Are Tennis Player Felix Auger Aliassime Parents? Everything To Know

August 2024 · 3 minute read

Born to Togolese and mixed Canadian parents, Tennis player Felix Auger Aliassime has advanced to the Quarters of the US Open after defeating Tiafoe.

Felix Auger Aliassime is a professional Candian tennis player who has now reached the US Open quarter Finals.

The game took place on Sunday night at Arthur Ashe Stadium where Aliassime drifted past Frances Tiafoe with the fourth round victory.

The game ended with the score of 4-6, 6-2, 7-6(6), and 6-4 in favour of Felix Auger, reports ATP Tour.

With consecutive quarter-finals entries in two recent grand slams, the Canadian athlete will be sure to move past his last failure in Wimbledon to reach his first-ever grand slam semi-finals.

Even though the player is still quite young, he is no rookie though. Aliassime became the second-youngest player to rank in the top 20 in men’s ranking in July 2021.

He ranked up at no. 15 in the ATP world ranking which he still holds.

Who Are Felix Auger Aliassime Parents, Sam And Marie?

The Tennis player, Felix Auger Aliassime was born to his father Sam Aliassime and his mother Marie Auger, as per his Wikipedia.

While the detailed background of his parents is still unknown, it is known that Felix’s father Sam immigrated from Togo and his mother Marie is of French Canadian descent, living in Quebec.

Sam and Marie seem to have met each other in Canada and got married in the late 90s.

Moreover, it was Felix’s father Sam who while working in a Tennis academy encouraged the athlete to get engaged with the sport and take the career.
