David Mulugheta Biography

David Mulugheta is an Eritrean-American NFL agent known for being the president of Athletes First which represents Aaron Rodgers, Derwin James, and Earl Thomas. He was born in the US to Eritrean immigrants. Mulugheta has represented at least one first-round selection for seven straight years.     David Mulugheta NFL Agent

    Mulugheta didn’t aspire to become an NFL agent. Growing up, he didn’t even play football much less know what a sports agent was. He wanted to be an entrepreneur or pursue corporate law. Howbeit, things turned around when he visited his best friend Jamaal Charles ahead of the 2007 draft, Texas running back’s agent, Andrew Kessler was the one who discovered him, Kessler revealed that he saw a 24-year-old he considered bright, loyal and authentic. he invited Mulugheta to intern with Athletes First that summer, ahead of Mulugheta’s first year of law school.
