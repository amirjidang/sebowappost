When the Harry Potter movies started being released in 2001, the whole world changed. It was groundbreaking and amazing at the same time! It opened everyone’s minds to the possibility of magic, intrigue, and so much more.

The cast of actors included many established performers and even more young stars, and their beloved performances only added to the success of the franchise.

Updated on February 8, 2022: The cast of the Harry Potter films reunited to celebrate the twentieth anniversary of Harry Potter and the Sorceror's Stone, and the reunion is now available to stream on HBO Max. Almost all the young stars made an appearance in the reunion special, and it is clear that, while they have all moved on with their lives, none of them will ever forget the experience of starring in this iconic franchise.
